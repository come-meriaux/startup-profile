What is our project?

The question was to integrate artificial intelligence into cosmetics. For this we imagined the installation of different sensors, two or three, in HighCosmetic stores. Customers carry out a test of a few minutes on these same sensors. It is at this precise moment that the AI intervenes by offering the customer up to three products adapted to his skin.

What is AI?

In a world increasingly developed and oriented towards robotization and automation. High Cosmetics bases its ambitions on a major innovation that will transform the future: artificial intelligence. According to Dominique Pastre, Professor of Computer Science at the University of Paris 5, "Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the science whose goal is to make a machine do tasks that man accomplishes using his intelligence."
